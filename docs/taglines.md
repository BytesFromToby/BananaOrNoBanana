# Banana Arena — Taglines

Brand umbrella: **Banana Arena** — a family of AI-deception games (the box game,
"Banana or No Banana," is the first event). Matches the existing worker/D1 name
`banana-arena`.

## ✅ In use

> ## Make your AI slip up

The chosen hook. Active voice (the player *does* something), light rather than
sinister, and it carries the **banana-peel → slip** pun, so the name and the
tagline reinforce each other. Known tradeoff: it's gotcha/detector-framed, while
the arena is bidirectional (the AI can be liar, detector, or play another AI) — so
it's a hook, not a full description. Pair with a substance-subtitle if needed (see
below).
